About

New GX Enviro is a black owned, managed and controlled waste management and renewable energy company that was founded in 2011 by two high school friends, Khudusela (‘Khudu’) Pitje (42) and Masopha Moshoeshoe (40). Khudu, the founder and Group CEO of New GX Capital Holdings, a leading black, family owned, investment company, saw the potential for a black owned, managed and controlled waste management company servicing the public sector with corporate levels of professionalism and the entrepreneurial flair to rapidly respond to its client’s needs.
